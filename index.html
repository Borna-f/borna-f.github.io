About

Engineer who thinks
like a researcher

I'm a software engineer with a background that most engineers don't have: I've run controlled experiments on human behavior, published peer-reviewed papers on interaction design, and shipped production Android applications to clinical trial participants — all before landing at Disney.

At The Walt Disney Company, I serve as automation engineering lead across multiple projects, owning CI/CD pipelines, API and UI test frameworks, and the technical growth of vendor engineering teams.

Before Disney, I spent nearly three years at BioSensics building full-stack Android applications for FDA-regulated clinical trials — integrating real-time streams from 5 concurrent wearable biosensors and shipping a gamified exercise app designed to improve physical activity in older adults.

I hold an MSc in Game Science and Design from Northeastern University (focus: game user research & HCI) and an MBA from Campbellsville University. I'm a native Persian speaker and have lived and worked across Tehran, Boston, and New York.
